Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Servings: Infinity stones
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Space Stone
  • Mind Stone
  • Reality Stone
  • Power Stone
  • Time Stone
  • Soul Stone
  • Avenger Stone

Instructions

  1. Begin by gathering all the 6 infinity stones
  2. Place the stones in an infinity gauntlet
  3. Activate the Power Stone
  4. Add the Reality Stone
  5. Infuse with the Space Stone
  6. Serve immediately (handle with care)
