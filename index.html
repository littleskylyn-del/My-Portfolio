Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Servings: Infinity stones
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Space Stone
  • Mind Stone
  • Reality Stone
  • Power Stone
  • Time Stone
  • Soul Stone
  • Avenger Stone

Instructions

  1. Begin by gathering all the 6 infinty stones, if any is missing, kinly check with Thanos
  2. Place the stones in a infinty gauntlet
  3. Activate the Power Stone to energize the cosmos
  4. Add the Reality Stone to suit your mission
  5. Infuse with the Space Stone to evenly distribute across the dimensions
  6. Serve immediately. effects may include universal balance or sudden responsibility over half the universe
